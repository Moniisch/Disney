Disney Glossary

Word Definition
Magic The feeling of wonder and excitement Disney creates in its stories and parks.
Castle The iconic symbol of Disney, often representing fairy tales and dreams.
Pixar A Disney subsidiary known for animated films like "Toy Story" and "Finding Nemo."
Mickey Mouse Disney's mascot and one of the most beloved characters in animation history.
Frozen A popular Disney movie featuring Elsa, Anna, and the song "Let It Go."
Hakuna Matata A Swahili phrase meaning "No worries," made famous in "The Lion King."
Disneyland A magical theme park where Disney stories come to life.
